## CONVERSATION
* W: Are you ready to order? 
* M: Yes, I would like to try the chef’s recommendation. 
* W: The roasted beef? 
* M: Yes, along with the onion soup. 
* W: How do you want your beef? 
* M: Medium-rare and no blood please. 
* W: Okay. I’ll notify the chef.
