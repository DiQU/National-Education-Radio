## CONVERSATION
* W: Hello, would you like to buy something? 
* M: Yes, that purse over there. 
* W: It is 20 US dollars. 
* M: Is it duty-free? 
* W: Of course. 
* M: Okay, I will have it. But make it fast please, I have a plane to catch. 
* W: Certainly.
